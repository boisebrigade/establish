208-334-4510 800-221-3295

Phone intake & application over phone, eligibility based on income & type of case. Free legal services for custody & visitation, divorce, debt collection, or modification of child custody court orders, guardianship child, guardianship adult, wills & estates, assistance for nonprofit corps. Priority is domestic violence and/or child endangerment cases. Will not handle criminal cases or any cash settlement.
